## Reliable Edge Protection and Fall Prevention in London

Falling from height is one of the leading causes of serious accidents in the construction industry. Our edge protection services in London provide a vital layer of safety for workers on roofs, leading edges, and open floor structures.

### Comprehensive Safety Barriers

We provide a range of edge protection solutions tailored to the specific needs of your site. Our systems are highly visible, easy to install, and provide a robust physical barrier.

- **Guardrail Systems:** Traditional scaffold-based rails or modular clamp systems.
- **Roof Edge Protection:** Specialized systems that attach to the eaves or structure of the roof.
- **Stairway & Slab Protection:** Handrails for stairwells and open floor slabs during the building phase.

### Why Prioritize Our Edge Protection?

We don't just provide a barrier; we provide a complete safety solution.

1.  **Fully Compliant:** Our systems meet BS EN 13374 standards for temporary edge protection.
2.  **Versatile Mounting:** We can install protection on concrete slabs, steel frames, and even delicate roof surfaces.
3.  **Rapid Deployment:** Efficient installation to ensure your site is safe as soon as work begins.

### Protecting Your Most Valuable Asset: Your Workers

Effective edge protection goes beyond compliance; it builds a culture of safety. Workers who feel secure are more productive and focused.

- **Integrated Toe Boards:** Preventing tools and debris from falling onto people below.
- **Adjustable Heights:** Ensuring the barrier remains at the correct height relative to the work surface.
- **Self-Closing Gates:** Providing safe access points for ladders and stairways.

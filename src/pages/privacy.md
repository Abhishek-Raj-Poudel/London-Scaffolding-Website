## 1. Introduction

London Scaffolding ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our scaffolding services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

- Name and contact information (email address, phone number).
- Property address for site assessments and scaffolding installation.
- Communication records when you contact us for a quote or support.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our scaffolding services.
- Process quotes and manage your bookings.
- Communicate with you about your projects and our services.
- Comply with legal and safety obligations.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our team is trained in data handling and safety protocols.

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

- Subcontractors or partners involved in delivering your scaffolding project.
- Local councils for the purpose of obtaining necessary street permits and licenses.
- Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate information.
- Request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
- Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
